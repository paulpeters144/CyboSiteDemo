Iode City

A city full of robots and humans attempting cohabitation. The Age of Intelligence began when robots started to reason, dream, and form their goals without the aid of humans. The Age of Intelligence ended just over a century ago, thanks to cybos - robots built alongside human ingenuity. However, the AI of a fallen world left a residue in archaic OS's still used in the present, threatening the chaos of the past century. Every passing day the question is still left unanswered. Will humans and robots ever understand each other? Is cohabitation is possible? Slowly, small glimmers of the answer form as the future and its technological advancements move closer and closer to the present.



The Game

Play the story mode and join a company to help in the struggle against the residue of AI. Help your company make a profit to protect it against the monopoly games of larger conglomerates. Face AI robots, or users sent from companies to gain an edge in the seemingly free market.

Or play the online mode and fight other players in tactical matches. Control your cybos and deploy strategies from your stratpack. Upgrade cybos with dependency chips, or hack opposing cybos to disrupt your opponent's strategy. In the online mode, you can climb the ranks of online play to become the best CyboShell user in the world.
